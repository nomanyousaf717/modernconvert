Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2025

Information We Collect

ModernConvert processes images entirely in your browser using WebAssembly technology. We do not upload, store, or have access to your images. Your files remain on your device throughout the entire conversion process.

Data Processing

All image conversions happen locally in your browser using client-side processing. Your images are processed by WebAssembly code running in your browser and never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, which helps us improve the service. This analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. No personal files, images, or conversion data are tracked or shared with analytics services.

Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense to keep our service free. Google may use cookies to provide personalized advertisements based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Google Ad Settings.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for analytics and advertising purposes only. No cookies are used to track your image conversion activities or store any of your personal files.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage analytics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • Vercel/Netlify: For website hosting

Data Security

Since all processing happens in your browser and no images are uploaded to our servers, your data security is maximized. We cannot access, view, or store your images because they never leave your device.
